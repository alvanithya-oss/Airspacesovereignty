The Resource for FAA 14 CFR Part 74 Rulemaking and Critical Infrastructure Airspace Policy

On May 6, 2026, the FAA released its proposed rule on Restoring Airspace Sovereignty (14 CFR Part 74), implementing President Trump's 2025 Executive Order. The proposal establishes a petition-based framework allowing critical infrastructure operators to seek restrictions on unauthorized drone activity near sensitive facilities.

AirspaceSovereignty.com provides updates, analysis, regulatory tracking, and compliance intelligence tied to this landmark shift in U.S. low-altitude airspace governance.
